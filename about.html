Our Mission

8 tools, zero tracking, just text.

NoteTxt was built out of frustration with over-complicated text fields that extract user profiles. We kept it simple: a clean workspace that saves directly to your device.

Behind the Code

Hi, I am Gabriel, the CEO and Founder of NoteTxt. When designing this workspace, my goal wasn't to construct another corporate note-taking conglomerate that prompts for credit cards or sells telemetry data. Instead, I wanted to deliver a crisp, lightweight, brutalist corkboard where developers, writers, and individuals can write quick memos, map diaries, or structure to-do lists without friction.

By shifting all operational weight onto local storage structures, NoteTxt ensures your text lines remain fully contained on your device. No backend architectures reading your files, no passwords to misplace, and no server disruptions.

Why NoteTxt?

  • Local Integrity: Your ideas belong exclusively to your browser sandbox.
  • Speed and Flow: No heavy external scripts loading trackers or slow cookie frameworks.
  • Complete Utility: Toggle dynamically between a diary tracker, a private journal, a string tool, and case configuration widgets without bouncing across separate domains.
