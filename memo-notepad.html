A Closer Look

What this memo notepad is for

A Free Online Memo Notepad

Memo Notepad is built for the thought you need to capture in the next five seconds, not the next five minutes. As a free sticky memo online tool, it skips titles, folders, and formatting in favor of a single board where a new memo is one tap away and saving happens automatically, no account and no install required.

Color-Coded Sticky Memos

Every memo can carry one of eight colors, so a glance at the board tells you more than the text alone. Use color for urgency, for category, or just for whatever system makes sense to you, the color-coded notes filter is built right into the toolbar for quickly narrowing the board down.

Pin, Archive, and Search

Pinned memos stay fixed at the top of the board no matter how many new ones arrive. Once a memo has done its job, archiving tucks it out of the way without deleting it, and the Archived view keeps it available to restore or remove for good. A search bar and color filter make finding one memo among many fast, even on a busy board.

Memo Notepad vs. Notepad Online

Notepad Online and Memo Notepad share the same instant auto-save, but they're built for different jobs. Notepad Online supports multiple long-form notes with titles, search, and a focus mode for sustained writing. Memo Notepad caps every entry at 280 characters and arranges them as a board of sticky notes, built for capturing a thought, not drafting one.
