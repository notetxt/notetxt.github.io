What it does

Every feature you actually need

No clutter, no subscriptions, no settings to wade through. Just the tools that make writing and thinking faster.

Core

Auto-save on every keystroke

Your note is written to localStorage as you type — no Save button, no lost work. Come back an hour, a day, or a week later and pick up mid-sentence.

Stats

Live word & character count

The status bar tracks words, characters, lines, and paragraphs in real time — useful for essays, tweets, cover letters, and anything with a target length.

Export

Download as a .txt file

Hit the Download button to save your note as a plain-text file to your device immediately — no account needed, no email involved.

Search

Find & Replace

Open the panel with Ctrl+H (or the toolbar button) to find any word or phrase and replace it across the whole note in one click.

Format

Text case converter

Switch the entire note between UPPERCASE, lowercase, and Title Case without leaving the page — handy for headlines, variable names, and social posts.

View

Dark mode & font size

Toggle dark mode for low-light writing sessions, and nudge the font size up or down to whatever feels right for your screen and your eyes.
