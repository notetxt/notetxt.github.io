Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours. Period.

Last updated: June 21, 2026. NoteTxt is built around a fundamentally simple principle: we do not want, collect, or store your personal documents, notes, or entries.

1. Local Storage Only

Any text, diaries, lists, memos, or selections you make within our suite of tools are kept locally within your web browser using Web Storage technologies (localStorage). None of your inputs are transmitted over the internet to remote servers or third parties. Since your data lives exclusively on your machine, it stays there until you decide to clear your browser cache.

2. No User Accounts

NoteTxt requires no registration, zero email inputs, and zero passwords to sync online. Because we hold no database infrastructure for accounts, it is structurally impossible for us to experience a server-side breach of your journal or diary texts.

3. Data Collection & Analytics

We do not capture user telemetry, keystrokes, or textual inputs. We may use standard web server logs or basic, privacy-focused analytical packages to review generalized, aggregated trends (such as total monthly visitors, geographical breakdown by country, and system browser varieties) to keep our services lightweight and functioning reliably.

4. Security Obligations

Because your content remains within your localized application cache, your security responsibility centers on keeping your operating system and web browser locked against unauthorized physical or electronic intervention on your specific device.

5. Third-Party Links

Our tools or informational footers might occasionally route you outward toward global web properties. NoteTxt possesses zero structural sway over external entities and assumes no data stewardship responsibilities when you vacate our ecosystem.
