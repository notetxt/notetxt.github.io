A Closer Look

Explore each tool in detail

Notepad Online

NoteTxt's online notepad is built for people who just want to write without opening a heavy desktop app. As a free notepad app with no sign-up, it works as quick scratch space for drafting emails, jotting ideas mid-meeting, or keeping a running list of thoughts you don't want to lose. Because notes online are saved automatically to your browser, you can close the tab and pick up the same notepad without login the next time you visit.

Diary Online

The diary online tool gives every entry its own date stamp, so your daily diary app naturally turns into a timeline you can scroll back through. It's built for the small, ordinary moments worth remembering, what happened today, how the week went, what you're looking forward to. As a free online diary, there's no journaling app subscription involved; it's simply a calendar of days, each one ready for a few honest lines.

Private Journal

Private Journal is the more guarded sibling of the diary, designed as a secure journal online for entries you'd rather keep behind a lock. With an optional password and a searchable history, this private journal app works well for therapy notes, personal reflection, or anything written for an audience of one. No account creation is required to use it, only the password you choose to set.

List Maker

The online list maker turns plain text into checkable items you can tick off one by one. As a free checklist app and to-do list maker, it suits grocery runs, trip packing, project task lists, or any plan that benefits from a satisfying strike-through. Items can be reordered by drag-and-drop, and multiple lists can be kept side by side for different parts of your life.

Memo Notepad

Memo Notepad is built for speed: a sticky memo online tool for the one-line thought you need to capture before it slips away. Unlike the longer-form notepad, this quick notes app favors short, color-coded memos you can pin and scan at a glance, closer to a real corkboard of sticky notes than a document editor.

Random Text Generator

This random text generator produces lorem ipsum and other dummy text generator output for mockups, layout testing, and design previews. Choose between random words, sentences, or full paragraphs and set the length you need, then copy the placeholder text straight into your design or document with one click.

Case Converter

The case converter handles the tedious part of reformatting text: switching between UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, and Sentence case in a click. As a text case converter built for both writers and developers, it also covers camelCase and snake_case, making it a handy uppercase to lowercase converter for headlines, variable names, and everything in between.

Decision Maker

When a choice keeps stalling, the decision maker tool offers a quick, neutral way through it. Use the built-in yes or no wheel for binary questions, or load your own custom list into the random decision generator to spin between options like restaurants, movies, or weekend plans. Each spin is logged so you can glance back at what was decided.
