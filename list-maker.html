A Closer Look

What this list maker is for

A Free Online List Maker

This online list maker turns plain text into checkable items you can tick off one by one. As a free checklist app and to-do list maker, it covers grocery runs, trip packing, project task lists, or any plan that benefits from a satisfying strike-through, all without an account or a download.

Many Types of Lists in One Tool

Rather than one generic checklist, List Maker offers seven starting points: a to-do list, shopping list maker, packing list maker, bucket list maker, goals list, reading list, and a blank custom list. Each keeps its own icon in the sidebar, so a quick glance tells you which list is which even with several open at once.

Drag-and-Drop Reordering

Priorities shift, so items don't have to stay where they were typed. Drag any item by its handle to move it up or down the list on desktop, or use the up and down arrow buttons, which work identically on a touchscreen, to keep the most important items at the top.

Your Lists Stay on Your Device

Every list, item, and checked box is stored locally in your browser rather than uploaded to a server. That keeps the list maker private by default: there's no login screen because there's no remote account behind it, just a set of lists that live on whichever device you created them on.
